Phase Three: Main Concept Idea

I would like to Design a quiz to find the most situable pet, I think the focus have to be in the life's style of the adopter more than the pet, because with this option you can cover more options with a more easy way than find a pet by breed, type etc, Also I have considered to add an advanced filter after the first quiz.

Selection of questions:

a) What is your house type?

You easily can filter if the pet is going to be alone or not meanwhile the adopter is working/busy, also if the pet have or need space to play, run, bathroom. etc, also you can choose if your house have a rural or urban style depending of the size.

b) Family members at house?

I have chosen this question because with this you can get, a range of age of the members of the house, if there are kids, seniors, if the adopter live alone etc, if the pet can fit in that family or person in terms of hyperactivity, love, interest.

c) How busy are you?

This option is good if you want to know how much time the adopter can spend with the pet everyday, some pets need a lot of attention (dogs) such as food, excercise, but another ones can take care by itself or need less attention (reptils).

d) Do you prefer stay at home or going outside?

Sometimes you are not really busy at work or you working from home, but you do not like to going outside a lot, So, this question is for get the average of pet that can be inside of home without stress.

e) Do you like doing exercises, any sport?

This item is good if you want to know if the adopters are able to have a pet with lot of energy, pets such as terrier dogs, or the ones that require lots of your attention.

f) Are you organized with your time?
